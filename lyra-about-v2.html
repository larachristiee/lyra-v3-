The story

Why I built
Lyra.

01

I wanted to build something on my own terms.

I started Lyra because I wanted to build something I was genuinely proud of — not clock in for someone else. But I also saw a real gap in the market that I couldn't stop thinking about.

02

Small businesses were being failed.

They knew social media mattered. They just didn't have the time to do it properly, and they couldn't justify the cost of a full agency. So they'd either wing it themselves or pay someone who treated them like a small fish in a big pond.

Neither option was good enough.

03

Lyra is the third option.

You get the professionalism of an agency without the overhead. Someone who actually learns your business, your voice, and what your customers respond to. Every month, clear proof it's working — in plain English.

The size of the client doesn't change the quality of the work.

How I work

THE LYRA approach.

01

Learn your business properly

Before we post a single thing, we learn what you do, who you serve, and what makes you different. Every strategy starts here — not with a template.

02

Build the strategy around your goals

Follower growth, enquiry generation, brand awareness — different goals need different approaches. We'll be clear about what we're aiming for and why.

03

Deliver consistently and report honestly

You'll always know what's happening. Monthly plain-English reports. If something isn't working, we'll tell you — and fix it.
