What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit — like whether you've already accepted a cookie notice — so you don't have to do it every time.

What cookies we use

CookieTypePurposeDuration
lyra_cookie_choice Essential Remembers whether you've accepted or declined cookies so we don't ask every visit. 1 year
_ga, _ga_* Analytics Google Analytics — tracks anonymised page visits, session duration, and general location. Helps us understand how people use the site. 2 years
_gid Analytics Google Analytics — distinguishes between users. 24 hours

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party marketing cookies.

Your choices

When you first visit our site, you'll be asked to accept or decline analytics cookies. Essential cookies (like the one that remembers your choice) are always on — they're necessary for the site to work.

You can change your preference at any time by clicking the button below, which clears your stored choice and shows the banner again on your next page load.
